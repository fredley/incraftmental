Assign

Choose a profession for . Your villager will start out as a novice, and level up over time. You can speed up levelling by building schools and universities.

Smith

This villager will be a smith, capable of smelting items faster and more efficiently than a furnace. Assign this villager smeltable items such as iron ore.

Builder

This villager will be a builder, who will automatically craft items for you. Assign this villager anything that can be crafted, and they will craft more of them for you.

Adventurer

This villager will be an adventurer. Assign them any item dropped by a mob, and they'll go and fetch more for you.

Chef

This villager will be a chef. Assign any food item that can be cooked and they'll do so automatically, and more efficiently.

Labourer

This villager will be a labourer. Assign them a tool and they'll use it much more efficiently for you.
